What is Escape?

In the fall of 2015, THEMEZIER released its first Weebly theme ever, Escape. While it wasn't a marvel, it did push boundaries for what a Weebly theme could do in terms of its design and features. ​Escape had sections before Weebly introduced the now popular "Sections" element and carried a design directly inspired by Google+.

​Unfortunately, as Weebly's UI evolved, Escape quickly became obsolete and would go away quietly, never to be heard from again.

​Until now, 2018 -- Escape is back and she has been entirely rebuilt from the ground up. Not only is she now a rock solid contender for your website, but she's had some major improvements and feature upgrades, while maintaining her iconic look. From Weebly 4's customizable header and "Sections" element, to a choice of 5 animations for her logo, Escape is primed and ready for your site!

Features

  • 100% ResponsiveYes, Escape is 100% responsive.
  • Weebly 4's Customizable HeaderWeebly 4's customizable header gives you a drag & drop banner area, which you can add a background image or solid background color to. You can also adjust the banner's height, or choose to make the background image parallax, reveal, or a video background (pro accounts only).
  • Weebly 4's Section ElementWeebly 4's section element allows you to create unique sections within the main content area. Each section is an independent drag and drop area that can be further customized with a background image or color, parallax or reveal effects, and a video background (pro accounts only).
  • Iconic LogoEscape's logo (site title) resides in the center of the page, hovering over both the customizable header (topper for no header page layout) and the slogan section. The logo is highly customizable and allows you to apply 1 of 5 different animations (i.e., bounce, flip, pulse, tada, and shake) courtesy of animate.css. You can also make the animations infinite, which means the logo will remain animated when the pages loads, or finite, in which the logo will animate just once every time the page is loaded. Additionally, you can customize the background and border color of the logo, or shut the background and/or border off completely. The logo will also automatically appear in the slide-on-scroll navigation and is also animated.
  • Slogan SectionThe slogan section resides just below the logo on every page. It houses 2 "Click Here to Edit" slogans that automatically appear in the slide-on-scroll navigation, as well as a global drag and drop area.
  • Slide-On-Scroll NavigationEscape's slide-on-scroll navigation is a navigation that drops down from the top of the screen -- on desktop, tablet, and mobile -- and houses both the site's menu, as well as the logo and the 2 slogans from the slogan section.
  • Accent ColorEscape comes with an accent color that you can choose under "Theme>Theme Options>Accent Color". The accent color is the color used by the buttons, navigation, social media icons, and active links.
  • Theme OptionsTheme options make it easy to customize Escape without touching any code. Escape's theme options include accent color, logo shape (i.e., circle or square), logo animation (i.e., bounce, flip, pulse, tada, and shake), infinite animation (i.e., the logo remains animated after the page loads), logo background color, logo border color, turn logo background and/or border off, no header's topper background color, navigation color, and the color of the tablet and mobile menu's toggle icon (i.e., hamburger).
  • 2 Page LayoutsEscape comes standard with 2 page layouts, a header and no header page layout.
  • Drag & Drop FooterEscape comes with a global drag and drop footer (includes free Weebly accounts). Changes made in the footer on one page will automatically reflect on all pages.
  • 100% Support & Free Lifetime UpgradesAs a user of this theme, we've got your back 100%! Though our themes are rigorously tested (they really are -- sometimes it takes days to iron out the bugs), if any issues ever arise with this theme, simply send us a message! You'll also enjoy free upgrades to the latest version of this theme for its lifetime. When new versions are available, you'll be sent a notification and download link via e-mail.
